Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: 28 May 2026

At Knock Out Pub & Grill, we respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle any information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data automatically through this website. If you voluntarily send us an order via WhatsApp or call us, we receive only the information you provide (e.g., your phone number, order details). We use this solely to process your order or answer your query.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To prepare your food/drink orders
  • To communicate with you about your order or booking
  • We never share your contact details with third parties.

3. WhatsApp Orders

Our basket system generates a WhatsApp message with your order summary. The message is sent via your own WhatsApp app. We do not store your order history on our servers.

4. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. The only storage is your current basket, which is saved temporarily in your browser and cleared when you close the tab.
